Top Manga Artists

Legendary mangakas who have shaped the world of manga with their groundbreaking work and storytelling.

Eiichiro Oda

Creator of One Piece, the best-selling manga series in history with over 500 million copies sold worldwide. Oda began One Piece in 1997 and continues to write it to this day.

Akira Toriyama

The legendary creator of Dragon Ball and Dr. Slump. Toriyama's art style and storytelling defined the shonen genre and inspired countless manga artists around the world.

Masashi Kishimoto

Best known as the creator of Naruto, one of the most iconic shonen manga series. Kishimoto's work has sold over 250 million copies and became a global phenomenon.

Manga By Era

Explore manga across different decades and discover how the art form has evolved over the years.

1990s

The golden age of shonen manga. This era gave us Dragon Ball, Slam Dunk, Yu Yu Hakusho, and the beginning of One Piece. Manga exploded in popularity worldwide during this decade.

2000s

The rise of Naruto, Bleach, and Death Note defined this decade. Manga became a mainstream global entertainment medium with diverse genres reaching new audiences everywhere.

2010s

Modern classics like Attack on Titan, My Hero Academia, and Demon Slayer emerged in this era. Digital manga reading expanded access for fans around the world like never before.
